What is CyberSecurity?

CyberSecurity is the umbrella term for a variety of niches that all share staying safe and preserving order in the digital world. Some niches include but are not limited to, Ethical hacking/Penetration testing, Digital Forensics, Cloud Security and Network Security. To become a CyberSecurity professional. You need skills such as patience, attention to detail and love of computers. Contrary to popular belief, you don't become a digital ninja, you instead sit behind the screen doing a lot of waiting and telling people to use better passwords and stop clicking random links. Yet there's a certain joy to it once you find your niche and get to work on it. Our demonstrative website has various activities built by students of the department and has short articles explaining their concepts briefly.
